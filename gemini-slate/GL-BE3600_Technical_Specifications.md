# GL.iNet GL-BE3600 (Slate 7) Technical Specifications

## Product Overview

**Model:** GL-BE3600
**Product Name:** Slate 7
**Type:** Portable Wi-Fi 7 Travel Router
**Manufacturer:** GL.iNet

The Slate 7 is GL.iNet's first dual-band Wi-Fi 7 portable travel router, engineered for on-the-go scenarios including business trips, vacations, and remote work. It combines high-performance networking with a compact, portable design.

---

## Hardware Specifications

### Processor & Memory
- **CPU:** Qualcomm Quad-core @ 1.1 GHz
- **RAM:** DDR4 1 GB
- **Flash Storage:** NAND Flash 512 MB

### Wireless Capabilities
- **Wi-Fi Standard:** IEEE 802.11a/b/g/n/ac/ax/be (Wi-Fi 7)
- **Frequency Bands:** Dual-band (2.4 GHz + 5 GHz)
- **Wi-Fi Speed:**
  - 2.4 GHz: Up to 688 Mbps
  - 5 GHz: Up to 2,882 Mbps
  - Combined Maximum: 3,600 Mbps
- **Wi-Fi 7 Features:**
  - Multi-Link Operation (MLO)
  - 4K QAM modulation
- **Antennas:** 2× foldable external Wi-Fi antennas
- **Concurrent Device Support:** 120+ simultaneous connections

### Wired Connectivity
- **Ethernet Ports:**
  - 1× 2.5 Gbps WAN port
  - 1× 2.5 Gbps LAN port
- **USB:** 1× USB 3.0 port
